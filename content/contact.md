title: Contact
---
To contact the Baha'i communities in Westchester County (and elsewhere), please call:

**1-800-22-UNITE**

They will direct you to the Baha'i community nearest you.

Most events listed on this site have a contact person — rather than calling the general number,
feel free to contact the event organizer directly. They will be happy to tell you about
the event and anything else you might want to know.
