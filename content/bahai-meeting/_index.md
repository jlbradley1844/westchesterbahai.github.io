This page lists Baha'i events for Baha'is. Most Baha'i events are open to the public,
but there are a handful of events attended only by Baha'is. These include Feasts, in which
we carry out discussions of community governance, and the yearly unit conventions where
deliberations take place at the national level.

*What is a Baha'i?* Informally, it's anyone who calls themselves Baha'i. Actual membership
(or *enrollment*, as it is called in the Baha'i community) is a formal declaration of
acceptance of the mission of Baha'u'llah, the prophet and founder of the Baha'i Faith.
