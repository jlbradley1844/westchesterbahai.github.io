## Official Baha'i Websites

* [Baha'is of the United States](https://www.bahai.us/)
* [Baha'i World Centre](https://www.bahai.org/)
* [Baha'is of New York City](https://bahainyc.org/)
* [Baha'i Reference Library](https://www.bahai.org/library/) — official repository for translated writings of the Baha'i Faith.
* Looking for West Chester, Pennsylvania? [Main Line Baha'i Community](https://www.mainlinebahais.org/) is probably where you want to start.

## Other Baha'i Websites of Interest

* [Baha'i Teachings](https://bahaiteachings.org/)
* [Baha'i Blog](https://www.bahaiblog.net/) — podcasts and more
* [Baha'i Library Online](https://bahai-library.com/) — critical website for Baha'i scholarship
* [Wilmette Institute](https://wilmetteinstitute.org/) — online courses in Baha'i-related subjects, open to anyone

* ["What is the Baha'i Faith?"](https://youtu.be/zLSaDVG4yBE) — a popular YouTube video introducing the Baha'i Faith, voiceover by actor Rainn Wilson

## The Internet

There is a lot of material on the internet about the Baha'i Faith. Much of it is by dedicated believers,
though there are others. As with everything on the internet, *caveat emptor*. The websites above
are accurate and reputable. For any subject — consider the source.
